Empathy map

Task of the assignment

Our group had to create an empathy map for our client that would give us an insight of his needs, thoughts, emotions and behavior.

What I Made

Our group created the empathy map based on the first interview with the client. During the interview we actively listened to the client and tried to pay close attention to his Verbal as well as nonverbal signals. We also asked questions allowing the client to freely express himself. After the interview, we carefully analyzed the information gathered and used it to populate the four parts of our empathy map - "Says", "Thinks", "Feels", "Does". In "Says" we documented statements and quotes from the client. In "Thinks" we wrote down his assumptions and beliefs. In "Feels" we captured the client's emotion state, while the "Does" part documented his actions and behavior. Me alongside my group members worked together on this assignment.

Personal contribution

Took part in discussing what should be putted into the different sections of the empathy map

Storyboards

Task of the assignment

We had to create a storyboard for the prototype interviews that would outline the key steps and interactions. The storyboard would also lead both the interviewer and the interviewee through the interview process.

What I Made

In order to create the storyboards our group firstly analyzed the feedback from the conducted interviews for the prototype of the webpage. We focused on understanding the user's perspectives and motivations. Then we began by sketching out frames of the main points that visually represent user's experience with the prototype. Each frame represent a specific step or action within user's journey capturing their thoughts, emotions and behaviors at each step.

Learning outcomes

The storyboards served us a tool for communicating and validating the design concepts as well as to visualize and iterate on the prototype based on the gathered information and feedback.

Heuristic Evaluation

Task of the assignment

We had to conduct a heuristic evaluation of our prototype for the client's webpage using established usability heuristics.

What I Made

Our group went through the website's design and functionalities comparing them to established usability principles and guidelines. We created a document that includes description of the identified problems and a recommendation for improvements as well as severity rating. rating.

Learning outcomes

This assignment helped us uncover usability issues such as unorganized categories or incorrectly presented information. By conducting a heuristic evaluation, we can identify and address these issues before users encounter them. It also provided us valuable feedback for optimizing the overall user experience.

AB Testing

Task of the assignment

We had to execute an A/B test to evaluate two different prototype designs and determine which one performs better in terms of user engagement, satisfaction, or any other relevant topics.

What I Made

In order to to this assignment we firstly identified the specific elements that we wanted to test, such as different layout of some elements, buttons and colors. Than we created two versions of the prototype each with variation in the chosen elements. Then we conducted the A/B testing session and gathered essential feedback on the design.

Learning outcomes

Took part in discussing which elements we should test.

Task Analysis

Task of the assignment

For this assignment we had to create a task analysis. The analysis aims to break down the navigation process into smaller steps, identifying key actions and decisions involved.

What I Made

Our team firstly identified the specific tasks that the users are most likely to perform on our webpage such as exploring the website or learning more about the artist's background. We deconstructed those tasks into sub-tasks. We created a document including triggers, main tasks and relevant desired outcomes.

Learning outcomes

This assignment helped our team to understand how users interact with our website and identify areas for improvements.
